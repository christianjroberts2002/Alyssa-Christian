Happy Valentines Day! The more time we spend together, I realize that Heavenly Father has placed you in my life right now for a special reason. You are amazing and I feel very privileged to have someone so wonderful as my person.

Birthday Note

Happy Birthday to the love of my life. As I have thought about what to write to you for the past month or so, each time I get overwhelmed by the love I feel towards you. I love you with all of my heart. Being with you these past 4 months officially (almost to the day) has helped me better understand a little bit more why a man and a woman need each other to gain exaltation. And I'm sure there's so much more to learn in the years we have ahead of us into the eternities. This greater understanding is hugely in part of your amazing example of righteousness, faith, trust, courage, and consistency. I know you aren't perfect and I don't expect you to be. I love the daily effort you give and I can say that you inspire me. I cannot express the happiness I feel that it's you that God has led me to. When we first started dating, I often expressed to my mom my fears and worries about marriage, with all it entails. However, after those feelings, a reassuring thought always followed and continues to follow; if it's with you, I can do it. You give me courage and strength that many times I cannot find on my own. No matter if it's learning how to openly communicate my feelings and thoughts or talking about sex and having babies and the ideal way to make out without being embarrassed, I know that I can do it if it's with you (sometimes it's not right away and I cannot tell you how much I appreciate your never-ending and love-filled patience with me). I love that you are a man who loves and knows your savior Jesus Christ. You have taught me so much about Him as you've shared through church talks, mission homecoming, mission prep classes taught together, and one-on-one discussions, plus Come Follow Me with your wonderful family. Because of this, you are a blessing in my life that is one I think Heavenly Father daily for. Your faith in Christ is a powerful tool that I've been able to witness in these past months and I'm excited to see it grow alongside mine. You are not only a huge example through your testimony but for other reasons
